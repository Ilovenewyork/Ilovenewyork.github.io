FREEDOM TOWER

The Freedom tower more commonly known as One World Trade Center is the TALLEST building in the western hemisphere and the 6th tallest in the world. There are great views from the 101st floor which is an indoor observatory which you can see most of New York from it also has a few restaurants but is mainly an office building.

Statue of Liberty

The Statue of Liberty is an iconic statue situated on Liberty Island gifted by the French as a symbol of America's independence from Great Britain

Central Park

A sprawling urban park, in fact it is the most visited park in the United States of America.Boasting a zoo ,carousel(one of the largest in the country),boat rentals,ballfeids and a resevoir it is an oasis of green in the middle of a bustling city

Times square

Sometimes called the crossroads of the world, it is famed for its giant electric billboards and a ball dropping down three feet from a pole on top of a building on New Years Day, but if you aren't a fan, there's always some dark alley to hide in.
